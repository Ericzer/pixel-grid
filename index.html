What Is This?

Pixel Block is a digital advertising experiment and creative billboard. You can buy space on a shared pixel grid and upload your image and link. Blocks are not permanent — they are rented. As long as no one else claims your space, your message stays up. Later, there may be a permanent phase.

Prices vary depending on the block size, so everyone can find an option that fits their budget — even the smallest blocks are accessible. This project supports creativity, visibility, and also helps fund my education and goals as a creator.

If you've found your way to this page but can’t buy a block right now, I would truly appreciate it if you could share this with someone — a friend, a relative, or on social media. Every bit of support helps this pixel wall grow.
